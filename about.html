About Glory Education Center

Equipping Servant Leaders for a Christ-Centered Future

Our Story

Glory Education Center (GEC) was established in 2012 in Yangon, Myanmar with a vision to raise up biblically grounded, spiritually mature, and practically equipped leaders. From humble beginnings, GEC has grown into a vibrant educational community offering degree and vocational programs to students from across the region.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission: To disciple and educate students with a blend of biblical knowledge, spiritual maturity, and practical leadership skills for kingdom service.

Our Vision: To be a leading center of biblical education in Myanmar, transforming communities through Gospel-centered leadership and holistic training.

Our Leadership

Rev. Samuel Tun

Founder & President

Rev. Samuel has a heart for discipleship and education. With over two decades in ministry, he established Glory Education Center to equip leaders who will serve in churches, missions, and communities across Myanmar and beyond.

“True leadership begins with a heart to serve.”

What Makes Us Different

  • All-inclusive facilities: Students receive hostel accommodation, food service, and WiFi access within the tuition structure.
  • Diverse academic options: We offer theological degrees (B.Th, M.Div, M.Th), music diplomas, and vocational programs in English, Hindi, computer, and more.
  • Faith-based learning: All programs are rooted in a biblical worldview with an emphasis on servant leadership and Christian integrity.
  • Scholarships available: Need-based and merit-based scholarships help make theological education more accessible.
  • Community and mentorship: Small class sizes and pastoral mentorship ensure deep spiritual formation.
